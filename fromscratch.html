Why I Chose Marketing

By Brooklyn Lowe

  1. It is essential to running a small business.
    • Understanding customers helps you attract more.
    • Knowing what a customer wants helps develop better products and services.
    • Knowing how to market the job to people attracts more valuable employees.
  2. BYU has one of the best programs in the country.
    • It is ranked 112th, making it in the top 15% of the programs in the country.
    • It is ranked #2 in Utah, which is a good location for me.
    • It is a great culture, with great connections.
  3. It provides lots of backup careers if my first choice doesn't work out.
    • I like interacting with customers but using data to do so, so I will enjoy any career in marketing.
    • Marketing is great for product management and development, which is interesting to me.
    • Brand management is also part of this, which interests me.
